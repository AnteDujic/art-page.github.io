ETNA CIYUD IS AN ART PROJECT STARTED BY AN AMATEUR ARTIST ANTE DUJIC IN 2020. BUILDING ON HIS CHILDHOOD PASSION FOR DRAWING HE TAUGHT HIMSELF TO DRAW AND PAINT. ANTE'S WORK MAINLY FOCUSES ON BLACK AND WHITE PORTRAITS, REPRESENTING HUMAN FRAGILITY WITH SUBTLE EMPHASIS ON UNSETTLING.
